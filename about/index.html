About Boise Tree Pros

Caring for Boise's Trees Since 2008

What started as a one-truck operation has grown into the Treasure Valley's most trusted tree care team. Our ISA-certified arborists bring science, safety, and genuine care to every job.

Our Story

From One Truck to Boise's Top Tree Care Team

Boise Tree Pros was founded with a simple belief: trees deserve expert care, and homeowners deserve honest answers. Back in 2008, it was just a pickup truck, a chainsaw, and a deep respect for Idaho's urban canopy.

Today, our crew of ISA-certified arborists serves hundreds of residential and commercial clients across Boise, Meridian, Nampa, Eagle, Kuna, Star, and the greater Treasure Valley.

We've never taken shortcuts. Every cut we make is informed by arboricultural science, every estimate is honest, and every job site is left cleaner than we found it.

Our Values

What Drives Everything We Do

Safety First

Every crew member is trained to the highest safety standards. We carry full liability and workers' comp insurance so you never have to worry.

Science-Based Care

We don't guess. Every pruning cut, removal plan, and treatment recommendation is grounded in ISA standards and current arboricultural research.

Genuine Care

We treat every tree like it's in our own yard. If a tree can be saved, we'll tell you. If removal is the safest option, we'll explain exactly why.

Honest Pricing

No hidden fees, no surprise charges. You get a detailed written estimate before any work begins, and the final bill matches — guaranteed.

Community Roots

We live here. Our kids go to school here. Supporting Boise's urban forest isn't just our job — it's how we give back to the community we love.

Clean Job Sites

We haul everything, rake every blade of grass, and leave your property looking better than when we arrived. No mess, no debris, no exceptions.
