Services

Boise Tree Pros provides professional tree care services throughout the Boise, Idaho metropolitan area and the Treasure Valley, including Meridian, Eagle, Garden City, Nampa, and Caldwell. Our services include tree trimming, tree removal, stump grinding, emergency tree service, tree health assessments, and tree cabling and bracing.

All work is performed by ISA-certified arborists following ANSI A300 industry standards.
